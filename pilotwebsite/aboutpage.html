About

This is a website for all people to come and enjoy reading and writing together. As an exercise collaborating with other writers is a great way to get out of writer's block or create something totally new and fresh. Working together will help improve skills like writing and teamwork, as well as being a fun way to meet other writers and get out of your comfort zone.
We hope that this website is used for all sorts of work, from professionals seeking advice, to educators enouraging teamwork in the classroom. This platform should be used in a positive and creative way. Writing is too often seen as a lonely thing to do and we want to change that. Collaboration is the best thing to help everyone get out of that rut, to meet new people, and read new things everyday.
To make things easier, we ask that you upload a pdf version of your writing to the server. This will make it easier for those to read, and when collaborating you just upload your own pdf that will show up with your partner's work, reading like a chapter.
