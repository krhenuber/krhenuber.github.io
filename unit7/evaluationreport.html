Summary of Evaluations

The design of the website seems fine to the evaluators, but they all said that the only picture just seemed to be there and held no purpose. They all liked my concept but said that it doesn’t seem to show up and there should be some changes made to the concept, which I agree with. ON the surface everything seems fine to evaluators, but Francesca points out that all of my links go somewhere else on my website and that nothing makes her want to return to the website. As for the fine details they all seem to agree that I missed out on all of them, and I agree. I think that they all want my concept to change, but it was a good one I just didn’t have the skills to make it come to fruition.

Cases

This website did not live up to the cases or my own expectations. I bit off more than I could chew and I didn’t realize it until now. I think with an updated concept I can make some new cases, such as a great resource still for teachers and other writers, just in a different way that makes it better.

The Changes

It can’t be an upload based website. So instead it will be a resource for people to learn how to collaborate effectively with others through a list of rules, outside videos, examples, and tips. This won’t take away the original concept completely but it is an overhaul I need to do in order to create website that I can manage with my skill set.

Reflection

I think that it helped me more to get feedback from them in this way because it helped me a lot more to understand what they wanted to see from my website. Jordan helped me when I asked about coding and he pointed out what I may need to change in that area. Jill helped me come up with a better concept and Francesca helped me overall with her comments on how to fix everything I was doubting. This was great experience so I can make what I want and also make it substantive instead of just surface level stuff.
