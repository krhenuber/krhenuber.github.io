Rules for Creative a successful expierence

Though you don't have to use them if you don't want to.

  1. Set an end date and create an outline before anything else.
  2. Know your characters and talk about their challenges as you go.
  3. Everytime you sit to write, let the other one know. They don't want to be surprised when they sit down to write.
  4. When there is a disagreement, set a way to get past this. My personal favorite is to let whoever wants the thing more to happen should win out.
  5. Update your goals as you go.
  6. Always be upfront, and don't keep secrets from each other about where you want this to go.
  7. Have an editing plan before you finish your first draft.
  8. Be good to each other, writing is fun and frustrating, don't get too upset over it.
