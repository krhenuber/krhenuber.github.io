Tips for Writing

Just some helpful tips for successful work!

  • Set some ground rules before you start. Here are some helpful rules to start from!
  • Try to write together on a shared document and physically together.
  • Know your strength beforehand, this will help with editing and piecing everything together.
  • Try to meet on neutral ground when writing if this is your first time working together.
  • Set a goal to finish by and let each other know if there's any changes ahead of time.
  • If you get blocked you have a whole other person to help with that, so always let them know you're struggling.
  • Don't do everything yourself. You're working together do you should slpit the work evenly.
  • Have a good attitude about writing, no one wants to work with a Debbie Downer.
  • Know when to stop writing. Together you should make the choice, but you should know when a good stopping point comes up.
  • Take notes of your editing process together. Compare them and start from there!
  • Be proud of what you did and what you do together!
