Welcome!

Weclome to Write with Me. a helpful resource for those whoe want to make meaningful connections in writing or struggling to successfully collaborate with others. On this website you will find helpful tips and a set of rules you can follow. There is also helpful videos and successful examples of collaboration.

One of the best reasons to collaborate with others is that it opens up more possiblities that yoy may not have considered before. Having a new perspective may totally change your story for the better. Other people have different styles, ideas, and even vocabulary, which in turn could lead to getting your work done quicker than you imagagined!
